Assumptions

  • The sample mass belongs to the stated pure substance or formula units represented by the molar mass.
  • Molar mass uses the same chemical formula and isotopic convention as the sample.
  • Concentration means amount concentration in mol per volume of final solution, not mass concentration.
  • The entity count uses the exact Avogadro constant, 6.02214076 × 10²³ mol⁻¹.

How it works

One mole contains a fixed number of specified entities. Molar mass tells you the mass of one mole of those atoms, molecules, ions or formula units.

Amount concentration divides moles of solute by the total solution volume. A concentration of 1 mol/L is 1000 mol/m³ in SI units.
