# Support

Thanks for using Ans. Support is handled personally — you'll be talking to
the person who built the app.

## Contact

Email: [support@anscalc.com](mailto:support@anscalc.com)

Include your app version if you can (Settings → About Ans shows it). Replies
usually come within a few days.

## Reporting a bug

The most useful bug reports include:

1. what you entered (the exact key presses, if possible);
2. what you expected to see;
3. what Ans showed instead;
4. your device and iOS version.

Calculation bugs are taken particularly seriously — if Ans ever shows a
wrong answer, please report it.

## Feature requests

Suggestions are always welcome. Ans deliberately grows slowly — features are
added when they make the app better to use every day, not to fill a list —
but every suggestion is read and considered.

## Frequently asked

**Does Ans work offline?** Yes, completely. It never uses the network.

**Where is my data?** On your device, and nowhere else. See
[Privacy](/privacy).

**Is there a subscription?** No. Ans is bought once and owned.
